Product Content
It all starts with the detail page
We need clean, professional photography. We need keyword infused copy to maximize search rank and traffic. And we need all detail page attributes to be filled out completely. Etailist includes in-house product photography and copywriting to all of our brand partners. We will ensure all of your detail pages meet our minimum content requirements, not just the top sellers.
Enhanced Content (A+)
Use A+ content to highlight your products' features, advantages, benefits
A+ modules allow us to use graphs, comparison tables and other digital assets to showcase why customers should buy your product. Etailist works with brands to design and build relevant A+ content to help maximize conversion.
Brand Store
Brand stores enhance the shopping experience for customers
Etailist creates and manages brand stores for all of our brand partners. Amazon Stores help highlight the brand's story and key differentiators, and can be used to create unique shopping experiences for promotional or seasonal purposes.
24/7 Monitoring
We are always watching (in a good way)
Etailist has proprietary software that monitors your detail pages 24/7. We scan for unexpected content changes, offer suppressions, hijackings and other issues, and will receive alerts when there are issues to investigate. Etailist also grades your content based on several data points, and displays a content scorecard in our partner portal. This allows us to track progress over time as we work to improve your detail page content.
