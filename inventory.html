INVENTORY OPTIMIZATION

WE USE PROPRIETARY ALGORITHMS TO MAKE SURE YOUR PRODUCTS ARE IN THE RIGHT PLACE AT THE RIGHT TIME
Purchasing
We send you consolidated orders - You ship to one location
We send purchase orders at wholesale prices and stock your products at our warehouse. We can place purchase orders via EDI, web portal or email. We do not ask for consignment inventory.
Forecasting
We rely on scientific approaches for on-time ordering
We have developed proprietary software to optimize demand forecasting and seasonality. We track in-stock and suppression metrics 24/7, and constantly communicate with our brand partners to overcome any supply chain constraints that may arise.
Item Prep
We report back to you with our 24/7 online portal
Our warehouse is fully equipped with the needed resources to prepare all your products for e-commerce success. We can handle re-packing cases to eaches, bubble wrapping, bagging, kitting, barcode labeling and more. We will work with you to define prep instructions for all sku's, and monitor Amazon returns and chargeback data to refine instructions as needed.
24/7 Reporting
Log in to our portal for 24/7 reporting
While we take care of every aspect of the business to make sure your brand wins on Amazon, our online portal is a real-time window into your business status and progress on Amazon. Log in to check metrics on inventory value, in-stock sku's, returns, aged inventory, and more.
